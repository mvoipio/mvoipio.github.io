A Lucet is a simple tool for making cord. This is a website on lucets and luceting, designed to help both beginners and those who are more advanced.

I am Mari Voipio from Vantaa, Finland, I make bands and cords as my hobby. I even teach luceting (and other techniques), and demonstrate luceting at various events. You can reach me by email at mari dot voipio at lucet dot fi.
