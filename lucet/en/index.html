A Lucet is a simple tool for making cord. This will be a website about luceting, an improved version of current www.lucet.fi, but the new English version isn't finished yet.

I am Mari Voipio from Vantaa, Finland, I make bands and cords as my hobby. I even teach luceting (and other techniques), and demonstrate luceting in various events. You can reach me by email at mari dot voipio at lucet dot fi.
