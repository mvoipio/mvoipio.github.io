Over the years I've tried all kinds of things, and some things worked and some didn't. This section contains a few tips I find handy.

  • Cutting a long cord into two and finishing off the ends can be done, if the yarn is thick enough: Cutting a cord
  • You should always aim at having enough yarn to complete the cord (10:1 rule). But if you do have to switch yarns, there are a few methods: Switching yarns
  • Have you heard rumors of a flat cord? It really is possible to achieve, although it is slower than basic square lucet: Flat cord
